ℹ About Us

Founded in 2010 by Grace and Samuel Kariuki with a single oven and a dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Over the years we have grown into one of Nairobi's most loved neighbourhood bakeries serving families, offices, schools and events with freshly baked products prepared every morning.

We believe baking is an art, not a factory process. Every loaf, cake and pastry is prepared using carefully selected ingredients sourced from trusted local farms around Nairobi.

Our mission is to create unforgettable bakery experiences through freshness, quality, creativity and exceptional customer service.
